01 The Property

A stone sanctuary
above the Adriatic

Casa Blu is a stunning 19th-century stone house that has been meticulously restored to offer the perfect blend of historic charm and contemporary luxury. Perched on a hillside overlooking the old town of Hvar, this exceptional property offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Adriatic Sea and the iconic city walls.

The villa features four beautifully appointed bedrooms, each with its own ensuite bathroom and spectacular views. The open-plan living area flows seamlessly onto a large terrace where you can enjoy al fresco dining while watching the sunset paint the sky in shades of gold and crimson.

The heated infinity pool appears to merge with the sea beyond, creating an unforgettable visual experience. Surrounded by Mediterranean gardens of lavender, olive trees, and bougainvillea, Casa Blu is truly a sanctuary of peace and beauty.
