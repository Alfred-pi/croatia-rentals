About This Estate

Casa Adriatica occupies the entire top floor of a beautifully restored 18th-century palace, just steps from Diocletian's Palace—one of the best-preserved Roman structures in the world.

The penthouse combines contemporary Scandinavian design with original architectural details, including exposed stone walls and restored wooden beams. Floor-to-ceiling windows flood the space with Mediterranean light.

The crowning glory is the private rooftop terrace, offering 360-degree views of Split's historic center, the harbor, and the islands beyond. Perfect for sunset aperitivos.
