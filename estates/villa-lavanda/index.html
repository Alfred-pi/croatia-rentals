About This Estate

Nestled among the famous lavender fields of Hvar, Villa Lavanda is a contemporary masterpiece that celebrates the island's natural beauty and legendary Mediterranean light.

The estate spans over 400 square meters of living space, with five luxurious suites each offering floor-to-ceiling views of the Adriatic. The design seamlessly blends indoor and outdoor living, with retractable glass walls opening onto expansive terraces.

The centerpiece is the stunning infinity pool that appears to cascade into the sea below, surrounded by native lavender, rosemary, and olive trees. A private dock allows direct access to the crystal-clear waters.
