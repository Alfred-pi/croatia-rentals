01 The Beginning

Born from a love
of the Adriatic

Founded in 2018, Adriatic was born from a simple belief: that the Croatian coast deserves to be experienced in the most extraordinary way possible.

What began as a personal collection of favorite properties has grown into Croatia's most prestigious portfolio of luxury villas — each one handpicked for its unique character, exceptional location, and unwavering commitment to guest experience.
