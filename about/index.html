Our Story

A Decade of Croatian Excellence

Founded in 2014, Dalmatia Estates has become the trusted name in luxury Croatian villa rentals, serving discerning travelers from around the world.

Passionate about Croatia

Our journey began with a simple belief: that Croatia's extraordinary coastline deserves to be experienced in equally extraordinary accommodations. What started as a small collection of three villas in Dubrovnik has grown into a curated portfolio of over 48 exceptional properties along the Dalmatian Coast.

Every estate in our collection has been personally visited and vetted by our team. We look beyond the obvious—seeking properties with soul, history, and that ineffable quality that transforms a vacation into a cherished memory.
