About Us

Our Story

Curating exceptional Croatian experiences since 2018

ADRIATIC was founded with a simple belief: that Croatia's extraordinary coastline deserves an equally exceptional approach to hospitality.

We curate a small collection of remarkable private villas, each selected for architectural distinction, prime location, and uncompromising quality. Our team handles every detail—from private chefs to yacht charters—so your only concern is where to watch the sunset.

Based in Dubrovnik, we bring deep local knowledge and personal relationships to create experiences that reveal the authentic character of the Adriatic.
